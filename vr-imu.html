Virtual Navigation using Inertial Measurement Units

  1. Home
  2. Portfolio

This project involves creating and navigating in a 3D world using an iPad. The accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer readings from the iPad are sent to a workstation over a wireless network and are used to determine the location and direction of motion in the 3D world. The scene is rendered on the workstation's monitor. The model of the university campus was created in Blender.

Team (2 members) Project for Virtual Reality and Navigation courses
